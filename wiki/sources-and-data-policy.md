# Sources and Data Policy

## Public By Default Only After Filtering

Compiled wiki pages may be public when they contain no credentials, raw private logs, local environment maps, personal memory dumps, browser/session data, or true local filesystem paths.

## Local Only

- `_raw/`
- private inbox content
- archives and restore material
- raw exports from chats, browsers, tools, or local applications

## Required Checks

Run structural and privacy checks before committing or pushing.
